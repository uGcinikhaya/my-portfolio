My Journey

From Estcourt to a Applications Specialist

Rooted in the values of my grandmother and mother, my journey spans from Estcourt and Mount Ayliff to my current professional path.

As I work toward having a strong career as a Clinical Pathology Biomedical Technician dedicated to always learning and striving to do more and beyond, I apply the same dedication to my transition into Business Analysis. This portfolio represents the bridge between my background in Clinical Pathology and my future in tech. I wasn't always sure where I was heading, because most of my high school joourney was spent being lazy and not taking my future very seriously but I've found my passion in bridging the gap between diagnostic precision and software innovation.
